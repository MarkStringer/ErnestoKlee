# ErnestoKlee

Is an idea I had for a novel. This man has pissed off some powerful forces in the Metropolitan police. So he's hiding out - kind of in plain sight - by house sitting for his friend and former sergeant who runs a security company.

So in each story, he's in a different house.

And of course, in each house there's a mystery or and adventure and he solves it.

What I want to do is not do anything creative with the ideas for the mysteries - like blues standards.

* Nazi gold.
* Identical twins
* She's in the attic!
* The theatrical company
* The money from the back job
* The reading of the will

But then, when you've got a standard mystery - like a jazz standard you can have fun with it. 

...that's the idea.
